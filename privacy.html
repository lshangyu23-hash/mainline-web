Privacy Policy

Mainline is designed as a personal life tracker that helps you keep daily actions, mainline projects, calendar plans, notes, and profiles within reach while you browse.

Data Mainline Stores

Mainline may store information that you enter into the extension, including todos, project names, notes, calendar items, special days, profile names, and completion history.

Where Your Data Is Stored

Mainline stores extension data using Chrome extension storage on your browser. The current version does not send your todos, notes, profiles, or calendar content to a Mainline server.

Data Sharing

Mainline does not sell user data. Mainline does not use your personal planning content for advertising. Mainline does not share your personal planning content with third parties.

Permissions

Mainline requests browser permissions so the extension can appear beside pages you browse, save your Mainline data, and open when you click the extension icon. These permissions are used to provide the extension experience.

Analytics And Tracking

The current version of Mainline does not include third-party analytics, advertising trackers, or behavioral ad targeting.

Your Choices

  • You can delete todos, notes, calendar items, and profiles inside Mainline.
  • You can remove Mainline from Chrome at any time.
  • You can clear extension data through Chrome browser settings.

Contact

For privacy questions about Mainline, contact the developer through the Chrome Web Store listing or the project website.
